Pinout wemos:
 ADC0 (A0) = LDR

GPIO4 (D2) = SDA RTC DS3231
GPIO5 (D1) = SCL RTC DS3231

GPIO14 (D3) = B led
GPIO12 (D4) = R led
GPIO13 (D8) = G led


GPIO0 (D6) = Relay Lampu
GPIO2 (D7) = Relay IN1 motor -> tersa mbung ke 2 trigger relay
GPIO15 (D5) = Relay IN2 motor -> tersambung ke 2 trigger relay

GPIO1 (TX) = Limit switch 1
GPIO3 (RX) = Limit switch 2

D0 = Input button
